MACCOLLAB – Image Folder
========================

Place your images here with these exact filenames:

  IMG_0837-HDR.jpg        → Hero section background (office interior, wide, ~1920×1080)
  building.jpg    → About section (building exterior, ~800×600)
  office-1.jpg    → Private Office card (1 desk, ~600×400)
  IMG_0861-HDR.jpg    → Small Office card (2 desks, ~600×400)
  IMG_0903-HDR.jpg    → Medium Office card (4 desks, ~600×400)
  IMG_0933-HDR.jpg    → Large Office card (8 desks, ~600×400)
  conference.jpg  → Facilities – Conference Room (~900×500)
  IMG_0852-HDR.jpg     → Facilities – Shared Kitchen (~900×500)

TIPS:
- Recommended format: JPG, optimized for web (compress to ~200–400 KB each)
- Aspect ratios matter: hero should be landscape widescreen
- Free stock photos: Unsplash.com, Pexels.com (search: "modern office", "coworking")
- AI images: Midjourney or Adobe Firefly work great for consistent style

If no images are provided, the site still works with CSS gradient fallbacks.
